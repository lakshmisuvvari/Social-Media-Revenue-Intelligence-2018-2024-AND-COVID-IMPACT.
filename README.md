# 📊 Social Media Revenue Intelligence · 2018–2024
### *Because data doesn't lie — but dashboards make it beautiful*

---

## 🚀 What is this project?

Ever wondered how much money Facebook, YouTube, Instagram and 
other platforms actually make from ads? And what happened to 
that revenue when COVID hit the world?

This dashboard answers exactly that — with data, visuals, and 
a story that unfolds across 2 interactive Power BI pages.

---

## 💡 The Story Behind The Data

In 2020, the world stopped. Businesses shut down. Ad budgets 
were slashed overnight. But social media? It didn't just 
survive — it absolutely exploded in the years that followed.

This project tracks that journey:
**From pre-COVID growth → the COVID dip → the massive post-COVID boom**

## 🔍 Insights That Stand Out

- 🥇 **Facebook dominates** — nearly 3x YouTube's revenue post-COVID
- 📉 **COVID dip was real but short** — revenue bounced back within 2 years
- 🚀 **Post-COVID = the golden era** — ₹5.14 Lakh Cr vs ₹1.25 Lakh Cr during COVID
- 📱 **TikTok rising** — fastest growing platform in the post-COVID phase
- 📊 **41.48% avg growth** — social media was the biggest winner of the pandemic era
